The Brief

Transform a traditional website into a modern Shopify store built for online sales and customer experience.

One Two One had an existing website built around their services and products, but it wasn't designed for e-commerce. The site didn't reflect a modern customer journey and lacked the functionality required to support online store operations, product browsing, and a structured purchasing experience.

Without the right e-commerce features, streamlined navigation, and a clear product browsing and checkout structure, online sales potential was being left on the table. Mobile experience was identified as a key priority — the platform needed to perform intuitively across all devices, particularly for handheld shoppers.

What We Did
01
Shopify Store Development — We rebuilt the website into a fully functional Shopify store, giving the business a live e-commerce platform ready to sell products online from day one.
02
Modern Website Redesign — We modernised the entire design and layout to create a premium digital presence that reflects the brand and supports a smooth customer journey.
03
Mobile Optimisation — We optimised the full experience for mobile performance, ensuring fast load times and intuitive browsing behaviour for handheld shoppers.
