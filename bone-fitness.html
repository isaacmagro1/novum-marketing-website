The Brief

Build a strong, consistent social media presence that reflects the energy, community, and intensity of the gym.

Bone Fitness Studio is a fast-paced gym built around structured classes, personal training, and a strong community culture. Their social media needed to capture that energy and bring it to life online, while maintaining the kind of professional consistency that builds long-term trust and credibility.

The existing content lacked structure, consistency, and a unified creative direction. Without professional photography, videography, and branded design, the online presence wasn't reaching its potential — leaving an active and engaged community underrepresented across digital platforms.

What We Did
01
Content Creation Direction — We directed and produced high-quality photography and videography to showcase the gym's environment, classes, and culture.
02
Branded Social Media Design — We built a consistent visual system and content templates that reflect professionalism while staying true to the gym's identity.
03
Social Media Strategy and Execution — We implemented a strategic content calendar and delivery approach designed to grow engagement and highlight community-driven culture.
